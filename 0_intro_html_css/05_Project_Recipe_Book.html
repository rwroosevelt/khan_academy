Recipe #1: Sichuan style Pork and Green Bean stir-fry

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
basmati rice 100g
green bean, topped and tailed, then halved 200g
sunflower oil 1? tbsp
minced pork 140g
dark soy sauce 4 tsp
rice wine 2 tsp
caster sugar 1 tsp
Sichuan pepper, lightly crushed using a pestle and mortar 1/4 tsp
red chilli, halved and sliced 1
fat garlic clove, finely chopped 4
small knob of ginger, finely chopped 1
spring onion, 2 finely chopped, 1 sliced for garnish 3
sesame oil 2 tsp

Step 1: Cook the rice following pack instructions. Boil the beans for 4 mins, then drain under cold water, pat dry and set aside.

Step 2. . In a large non-stick wok or frying pan, heat 1 tbsp of the oil. Add the pork mince and fry for a few mins, breaking up into small pieces with the back of a spoon. Stir in half the soy, the rice wine and sugar and cook for 30 secs more. When the meat is cooked through, tip onto a plate.

Step 3. Heat the remaining oil in the pan. Add the pepper, chilli, garlic, ginger and chopped onions, and stir-fry for 2 mins until ginger and garlic are softened. Tip in the beans, heat through, then add the mince ? stir-fry for 2 mins until hot. Stir in the rest of the soy and sesame oil. Scatter with spring onions and serve with rice.

Recipe #2: Sichuan pork, broccoli & cashew stir-fry

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
vegetable oil 1 tbsp
pork fillet, cut into thick slices 500g
garlic clove, thinly sliced 1
Tenderstem broccoli, cut into 3cm pieces 300g
red onion, cut into thick half moons 2
toasted unsalted cashew 50g
SAUCE
Dark soy sauce 1 tbsp
Chinese black or red wine vinegar 1 tbsp
Rice wine or sherry 1 tbsp
Chilli bean sauce or chilli sauce 1 heaped tbsp
Liquid chicken stock 1 tbsp
Golden caster sugar 1 tbsp
Cornflour dissolved in 2 tbsp water 2 tbsp

Step 1: In a small bowl, mix together the sauce ingredients and set aside. Heat the oil in a large wok. Season the pork and sear in batches, then remove and set aside.

Step 2: Fry the garlic until golden ? about 30 secs ? then add the broccoli and onions. Stir-fry for 2 mins, then add the pork and cashews along with the sauce. Keep stir-frying for another 2-3 mins until everything is hot and simmering.

Recipe #3: Spicy Sichuan-style prawns

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Cook Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Groundnut oil 1 tbsp
Ginger, finely chopped 2cm piece
Garlic cloves, coarsely chopped 2
Spring onion, finely chopped 1
Raw prawns, shelled and de-veined 450g
SAUCE
Tomato puree 1 tbsp
Chilli bean sauce 3 tbsp
Chinese black vinegar or cider vinegar 2 tbsp
Golden caster sugar 2 tbsp
Sesame oil 2 tbsp
Coriander leaves handful
Sliced Onions to serve

Step 1: Heat a wok over a high heat. Add the groundnut oil and, when it is very hot and slightly smoking, add the ginger, garlic and spring onions. Stir-fry for 20 secs, then add the prawns. Stir-fry for about 1 min, then add all the sauce ingredients, along with ? tsp salt and ? tsp ground black pepper. Continue to stir-fry for another 3 mins over a high heat. Serve at once, scattered with the coriander and spring onion.
